## <a name="create-a-hierarchy"></a><span data-ttu-id="61108-112">Créer une hiérarchie</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-112">Create a hierarchy</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="61108-113">Accédez au **volet de navigation > Modules > Administration d’organisation > Organisations > Hiérarchies de l’organisation**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-113">Go to **Navigation pane > Modules > Organization administration > Organizations > Organization hierarchies**.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="61108-115">Tapez une valeur dans le champ **Nom**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-115">In the **Name** field, type a value.</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="61108-116">Dans la section **Objectif**, cliquez sur **Affecter un objectif**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-116">In the **Purpose** section, click **Assign purpose**.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="61108-117">Dans la liste, recherchez et sélectionnez l’enregistrement souhaité.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-117">In the list, find and select the desired record.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="61108-118">Sélectionnez un objet à affecter à la hiérarchie d’organisation.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-118">Select a purpose to assign to your organization hierarchy.</span></span>

7. <span data-ttu-id="61108-120">Dans la liste, marquer la ligne sélectionnée.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-120">In the list, mark the selected row.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="61108-121">Recherchez la hiérarchie que vous venez de créer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-121">Find the hierarchy you just created.</span></span>  
8. <span data-ttu-id="61108-122">Cliquez sur **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="61108-122">Click **OK**.</span></span>
