## <a name="how-master-planning-handles-the-safety-stock-constraint"></a><span data-ttu-id="edef2-208">Procédure de gestion de la contrainte de stock de sécurité par la planification</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="edef2-208">How master planning handles the safety stock constraint</span></span>
