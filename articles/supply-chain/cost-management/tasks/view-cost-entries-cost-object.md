1. <span data-ttu-id="141c7-107">Cliquez sur Administration des coûts.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141c7-107">Click Cost administration.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="141c7-108">Cliquez sur Produits lancés.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141c7-108">Click Released products.</span></span>

6. <span data-ttu-id="141c7-113">Cliquez sur Entrées de coût.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="141c7-113">Click Cost entries.</span></span>
