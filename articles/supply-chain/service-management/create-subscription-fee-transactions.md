[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="5f4db-104">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Commun** \> **Services récurrents** \> **Tous les abonnements aux services**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5f4db-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>
