[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="8deae-104">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Commun** \> **Services récurrents** \> **Tous les abonnements aux services**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8deae-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="8deae-105">Dans la page de liste **Tous les abonnements aux services**, sélectionnez l'abonnement pour lequel vous souhaitez créer une transaction de frais d'abonnement.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8deae-105">On the **All service subscriptions** list page, select the subscription for which you want to create a subscription fee transaction.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="8deae-108">Cliquez sur **OK**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="8deae-108">Click **OK**.</span></span>
