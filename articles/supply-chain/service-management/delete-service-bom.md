# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="00dc3-103">Suppression d’une nomenclature des services</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-104">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Commun** \> **Accords de service** \> **Accords de service**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-105">Sélectionnez l’accord de service duquel vous souhaitez supprimer une nomenclature des services.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-106">Dans le volet **Actions**, cliquez sur **Objets du service**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-107">Dans l’écran **Objets du service**, sélectionnez l’objet de service duquel vous souhaitez supprimer une nomenclature des services.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-108">Cliquez sur **Fonctions**, puis sélectionnez **Supprimer la nomenclature des services**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-109">Cliquez sur **Oui**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="00dc3-110">Si vous n’avez pas appliqué de nomenclature des modèles, vous pouvez supprimer une nomenclature des services dans l’écran <STRONG>Nomenclatures des modèles</STRONG>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-110">If you have not applied a template BOM, the service BOM can be deleted from the <STRONG>Template BOMs</STRONG> form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="00dc3-111">Si vous avez appliqué une nomenclature des modèles, vous ne pouvez pas supprimer la nomenclature des services à l’aide de cette méthode.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="00dc3-111">If you have applied a template BOM, the service BOM cannot be deleted by using this method.</span></span></P>
