# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="9f7da-103">Suppression d'une nomenclature des services</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="9f7da-104">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Commun** \> **Accords de service** \> **Accords de service**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9f7da-105">Sélectionnez l'accord de service duquel vous souhaitez supprimer une nomenclature des services.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="9f7da-106">Dans le volet **Actions**, cliquez sur **Objets du service**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="9f7da-107">Dans l'écran **Objets du service**, sélectionnez l'objet de service duquel vous souhaitez supprimer une nomenclature des services.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="9f7da-108">Cliquez sur **Fonctions**, puis sélectionnez **Supprimer la nomenclature des services**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="9f7da-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>
