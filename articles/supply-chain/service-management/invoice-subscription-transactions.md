# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="f19f5-103">Facturation de transactions d’abonnement</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-104">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Commun** \> **Services récurrents** \> **Tous les abonnements aux services**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-105">Sélectionnez un service récurrent, puis cliquez sur **Modifier** ou sur **Abonnement** pour créer un service récurrent.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-105">Select a service subscription and then click **Edit** or click **Subscription** to create a service subscription.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-106">Cliquez sur **Analyser** \> **Transactions de frais**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-107">Sélectionnez la ou les transactions de frais à facturer, puis cliquez sur **Fonctions** \> **Proposition de facture** pour ouvrir l’écran **Propositions de facture**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-110">Vérifiez le contenu de la facture, puis cliquez sur **Valider la facture** pour ouvrir l’écran **Valider la facture** pour valider la facture.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f19f5-111">Vous pouvez également imprimer la facture à partir de l’écran **Valider la facture**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f19f5-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
