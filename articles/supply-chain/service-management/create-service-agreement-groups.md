[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]

<span data-ttu-id="2d25b-104">Les groupes d'accords de service permettent de trier et de filtrer les accords de service.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d25b-104">Service agreement groups are useful for sorting and filtering service agreements.</span></span>

1. <span data-ttu-id="2d25b-105">Cliquez sur **Gestion des services** \> **Paramétrage** \> **Accords de service** \> **Groupes d'accords de service**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d25b-105">Click **Service management** \> **Setup** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreement groups**.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="2d25b-106">Appuyez sur **Alt+N** pour créer un groupe d'accords de service.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2d25b-106">Press **Alt+N** to create a new service agreement group.</span></span>
